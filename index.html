Afraid of Flying?

The probability of a plane crash is about one per 1.2 million flights, so its pretty unlikely you'll be in one. Regardless, we looked at almost 2000 accidents to examine what factors make it more likely.

Make and Model of Aircraft

Below you can see the number of accidents per make. The bars represent the number of accidents from the years 1995 to 2016, colored by red for fatal, orange for serious injuries, and yellow for incident. Phase of the Flight

There are some phases of a flight where a passenger is less likely
to get a serious or fatal injury in comparison to other phases.

The red squares represent the percentage of passengers with reported fatal injuries at a specific flight phase out of all passengers on flight incidents from the years 1995 to 2016.

The orange squares represent the percentage of passengers with reported serious injuries at a specific flight phase out of all passengers on flight incidents from the years 1995 to 2016.
